2. Actively searching

With enough information about the relative and where they are, we can start actively looking for the relatives.

Volunteers in our international network visit the last known locations, ask questions and check records to find families.

It's public: we share your info with people who can help us find your family.

It takes time: searches can take months, but often take years.

It's thorough: we visit villages and camps, send messages & check records.

This service is right for people who:

Feel safe if we publicise the search, ask questions and check public records.

Have enough information about their relatives and where they might be.

Are looking in a country that is currently accepting enquiries.
